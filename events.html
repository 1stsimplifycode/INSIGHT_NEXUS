Smart India Hackathon 2023

Smart India Hackathon 2023 is a nationwide initiative launched by AICTE and MoE to provide students with a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives. The event aims to inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving among young and innovative minds. The hackathon invites students from classes 6th to 12th to showcase their out-of-the-box thinking and innovative ideas.
